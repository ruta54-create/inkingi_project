About Inkingi Wood

Crafting Excellence, Connecting Artisans, Preserving Tradition

Our Story

Inkingi Wood was born from a deep-rooted passion for Rwandan craftsmanship and sustainable woodwork. Founded in 2023, we bridge the gap between skilled artisans and discerning customers worldwide.

Our platform celebrates Rwanda's rich woodworking heritage while embracing modern e-commerce. We empower local craftsmen, preserve traditional techniques, and deliver exceptional wood products that tell a story of craftsmanship and sustainability.

Sustainably sourced Rwandan wood
Supporting 50+ local artisans
Quality-certified craftsmanship

Sustainability

We exclusively use wood from responsibly managed forests and promote reforestation initiatives across Rwanda.

Community Impact

Empowering local artisans with fair wages, training programs, and access to global markets.

Quality Assurance

Every product undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure exceptional craftsmanship and durability.
